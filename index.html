Services

At Bat's Better, we manufacture custom parts to the highest degree of quality. We pride ourselves on being able to quickly and cost-effectively make any part you request, from scateboard trucks to rocket parts (for real).

High Precision

All of our machines can hold tolerance down to a 0.0001 inches or 0.0025 mm. Designs can be specified in either fractional or metric systems.

Variety of Materials

We work daily with a wide variety of materials, from aluminum to plastics and more.
